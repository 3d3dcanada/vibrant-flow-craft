### Editorial Safety & Defamation Policy

**3D3D does not accuse specific sellers, brands, or individuals of fraud unless:**

1. Evidence is documented and verifiable
2. Claims are framed as **risk indicators**, not allegations
3. Language is **comparative and educational**, not accusatory

**We NEVER imply:**
- ‚ùå Criminal intent
- ‚ùå Illegal behavior
- ‚ùå Deliberate deception

**We ALWAYS frame as:**
- ‚úÖ Observed patterns
- ‚úÖ Consumer risk factors

## Pillar: Marketplace Awareness (Legally Safe)

### Visual Guide: Quality Indicators

**Indicators of Higher-Quality 3D Prints:**
- ‚úÖ Clean layer lines (proper print settings)
- ‚úÖ Consistent color (quality filament)
- ‚úÖ Flat base (proper bed preparation)
- ‚úÖ No stringing (tuned retraction)
- ‚úÖ Neutral or mild PLA scent (standard materials)
- ‚úÖ Appropriate weight (structural integrity)
- ‚úÖ Maker attribution (accountability)

**Risk Factors That May Indicate Lower Quality:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Visible layer lines (may indicate rushed printing)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Inconsistent color (may indicate variable filament quality)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Warping/curling (may indicate adhesion issues)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Stringing between parts (may indicate lack of quality control)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Strong chemical odor (may warrant material safety questions)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Unusually light weight (may indicate thin walls/low infill)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è No maker information (limits accountability)

**Framing**: Observations, not accusations.
